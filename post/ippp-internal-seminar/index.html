IPPP internal seminar: NLO QCD corrections to diphoton-plus-jet production through gluon fusion at hadron colliders

   14 Jan 2022      1 min read      physics      durham · internal · ippp · phd · seminar · talk

Talk in the IPPP internal seminar series, Durham

I gave a talk in the IPPP internal seminar series about my recent work on diphoton-plus-jet production via gluon fusion, including:

  • Next-to-leading order QCD corrections to diphoton-plus-jet production through gluon fusion at the LHC
  • Virtual QCD corrections to gluon-initiated diphoton plus jet production at hadron colliders
  • Optimising simulations for diphoton production at hadron colliders using amplitude neural networks
