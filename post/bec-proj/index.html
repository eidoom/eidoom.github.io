Polarisation in photon Bose-Einstein condensation

   17 Oct 2016      1 min read      physics      st andrews · undergrad

Summer research project on a macroscopic quantum phenomenum

My second summer research project was with Jonathan Keeling and Peter Kirton, modelling the polarisation behaviour of a Bose-Einstein condensate of photons. I wrote a Python code to simulate the system for this study. I reported (source) on the work at the end of the summer.

In the next academic term, I gave a presentation (source) to undergraduates in my department. I also continued work on the project, leading to a paper (arXiv).
